Software Developer

What is a software developer?

A software developer as the name suggests develops apps and programs that help your electronic device run better. They make sure that no viruses are in your device, and that your experience with computers is at it's fullest potential.

What is the software/hardware used in this job?

The programming language most developers use are C, C++, Java, and Python. The hardware mostly used in programming computers is Intel i5 CPU, with an i7 recommended. 8GB RAM, with 16GB recommended. 1920 x 1080 resolution display.

What is the salary of a software developer?

In 2018 the salary of a software developer ranged from $66,740-$166,960.

What are the skills required to be a software developer?

Being a software developer requires you to be problem solving, a team player, analytical, strategic, and have intrinsic motivation.

What is the future of a software developer?

The future of software developers looks like the job is becoming available for more people as time goes on.
